WHAT IS IT ?
Blockchain Search Engine is your all-one-search engine to search data, address, transactions across all popular blockchains in the world. You also get an extensive directory of all blockchain or cryptocurrency related websites, blogs and channels.
A BRIEF HISTORY & WHY YOU NEED THIS WEBSITE
This website has been created on April 2018 due to the requirement of searching data, transaction history etc. across different blockchains. Since there are over 1000 blockchains in the world it's quite essential to have a single website to search across any blockchain. So use it and feel free to drop in your suggestions to make this website even better :-)
WHAT CAN YOU DO WITH THIS WEBSITE
You can do the following things on this website :-
  • Search the blockchain for over 800 Cryptocurrency Addresses.
  • Search transactions on the blockchain for any cryptocurrency.
  • Search block data & history for any cryptocurrency on the blockchain.
  • Search and use the directory of cryptocurrency and blockchain related websites, news channels, groups and communities.
  • Check out the basic guide on blockchain and cryptocurrencies for beginners if you are new to this space.
